# How it works

## First time you open SANG

You're greeted with a short welcome screen that asks for three things:

1. Your **name**
2. Your **phone number**
3. Your **email**

Once you save them, SANG remembers you and takes you straight into the app. You
won't be asked again — your card is ready.

## Sharing your details (you are the sharer)

Open the **My Card** tab. You'll see your personal QR code along with your name and
details on a clean card. Hold your phone up so the other person can scan it, or use
the **Share** button to send the code as an image through any messaging app.

You can tap **Edit** at any time to update your name, number, or email — your QR
code updates automatically.

## Saving someone else (you are the scanner)

Open the **Scan** tab and point your camera at another person's SANG QR code. The
moment it recognises the code, SANG reads their details and saves them into your
**Contacts** list. Their full profile opens right away so you can confirm you got
the right person.

## Your collected contacts

The **Contacts** tab keeps everyone you've scanned in one tidy list. Tap any person
to see their full details, where you can:

- Tap their number to **call** them
- Tap their email to **send a message**
- Remove them if you no longer need the contact

## Works with any camera too

Because SANG uses the standard format that phones already understand for contacts,
your QR code also works when scanned by a regular phone camera or any other QR
scanner — it will simply show the contact details. SANG just makes the experience
richer by saving and organising them for you.
