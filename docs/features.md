# Features

## Your personal QR card
- Enter your name, phone number, and email once.
- SANG turns them into a scannable QR code instantly.
- The card shows your details in a clean, shareable layout.

## Easy editing
- Update your name, number, or email whenever you like.
- The QR code refreshes automatically to match.

## Share anywhere
- Send your QR code as an image through messaging, email, or social apps.
- Show it on your screen for someone to scan in person.

## Fast scanning
- Point the camera at a QR code and SANG recognises it automatically.
- The scanned person is saved and their profile opens right away.

## Organised contacts
- Every person you scan is collected in one list.
- Tap a contact to call or email them directly.
- Remove contacts you no longer need.

## Works offline
- No internet connection required to create, share, or scan codes.
- No sign-up and no account.

## Compatible with any scanner
- The QR code follows the standard contact format, so even a regular phone
  camera can read it as a contact.

## Light and dark mode
- The app automatically matches your phone's appearance settings.
