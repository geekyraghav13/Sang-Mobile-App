# Overview

## What is SANG?

SANG is a digital business card that lives on your phone. When you first open the
app, you enter your **name**, **phone number**, and **email**. SANG instantly turns
those details into a personal **QR code**.

Anyone can scan that code to save you as a contact — in seconds, with no mistakes,
and without you having to read your number out loud or hand over a paper card.

## Who is it for?

- **Networking** — meet someone at an event and share your details with a single scan.
- **Small businesses & freelancers** — put your QR on a phone screen, a poster, or a name tag.
- **Anyone** who is tired of typing contact details into a phone by hand.

## The two people in every exchange

SANG is built around a simple idea: two people, each with the app.

- **The sharer** shows their QR code (their "My Card").
- **The scanner** points their camera at it, and the sharer's details land safely in
  the scanner's saved contacts.

Both roles live in the same app, so everyone can both share and collect.

## Why a QR code?

A QR code is just a picture that holds information. SANG packs your contact details
directly into that picture, so the code itself carries everything needed — no
internet connection, no account, and no waiting. Scan it and the details appear.
