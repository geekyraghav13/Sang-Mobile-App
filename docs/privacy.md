# Privacy

SANG is designed to keep your information in your hands.

## Everything stays on your phone
- Your card details (name, number, email) are stored only on your own device.
- The contacts you scan are saved only on your device.
- Nothing is uploaded to a server, and there is no SANG account.

## No internet needed
- Creating your card, sharing it, and scanning others all work fully offline.

## What's inside your QR code
- Your QR code contains exactly the details you entered: your name, phone number,
  and email. Anyone who scans it sees those details — which is the whole point of
  sharing a contact card. Only share it with people you're happy to give your
  details to.

## Camera use
- The camera is used only while you're on the Scan screen, and only to read QR
  codes. SANG asks for camera permission the first time and you can decline or
  revoke it in your phone's settings at any time.

## Removing data
- Delete any saved contact from its detail screen.
- Removing the app from your phone removes all of its stored data.
